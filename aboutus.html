About us

Legends barber is a barbershop established back in 2011.
It was founded by Sheldon Tatchell who is also the CEO of the franchise.
We specialise in haircuts such as fades and chiskop for both men and women.
Legends barber also provides services such as manicure, pedicure and hairwashing for ladies.
We also provide a mobile service to keep up with the lifestyle of some of our high profile clients.

What keeps us going?

Making our clients happy with our quality services
and seeing them keep coming back to us is what keeps us going as Legends Barber.
